ANIMAL rescue

Fortunate homeless and uncared for animals end up in this animal rescue shelter that are staffed by professional, caring people.
At this facility, frightened animals are reassured, sick and injured animals receive treatment or a peaceful end to their suffering, and the animals’ living quarters are kept clean and dry.
Workers at these facilities never turn away needy animals and give careful consideration to each animal’s special emotional and physical needs.
If you come across an injured animal, you can help them by find a shelter nearby location .
